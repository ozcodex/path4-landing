PATH4 - Next level community-driven public transport information.
Unreliable information sucks. Path 4, with its strong community of commuters, is delivering you a real-time picture of the public transport information for the city of Munich.

But how does it work?!

By connecting the dots of transportation information to the eyes of thousands of daily travellers, the service is empowered to be the most precise public transport app. We collect real-time data from our users (on a voluntary basis) to enrich the publicly available data sets.

And what do I get out of it?

Quite simple: a fully relaxed public transport experience!

You will be provided with the most accurate departure and arrival data, with crowdedness information of your chosen subway, tram or bus and with exit information of your target destination, so that you will never use a wrong exit again!
